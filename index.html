Services

Dispute Resolution Services

Comprehensive resolution across every major forum, tailored to the complexity and value of each matter.

01 Expert Determination
Independent expert determination on technical matters — binding or non-binding, efficient, confidential, and cost-effective resolution.
02 Commercial Arbitration
Streamlined arbitration delivering binding, enforceable awards for construction and commercial disputes with fixed-cost schemes available.
03 Commercial Mediation
Facilitated negotiation with deep construction expertise, enabling confidential voluntary settlements at lower cost and shorter timeframes.
04 Dispute Boards
Standing and ad hoc boards for proactive dispute management through early intervention and continuous engagement throughout the contract period.
05 Conciliation
Independent conciliation combining facilitative and evaluative approaches, with informed assessment and settlement proposals where appropriate.
06 Expedited Arbitration
Streamlined procedure for disputes not exceeding USD 1,000,000 — final awards within six months with full due process safeguards.
Process

How IDRS Works

A structured approach guiding parties from initial engagement through to final resolution.

Step 01

Pre-Filing Strategy

Initial assessment and strategy formulation, identifying the most appropriate resolution forum and procedural approach.

Step 02

Discovery & Investigation

Thorough investigation of technical and commercial matters, gathering evidence and analysing quantum, delay, and causation.

Step 03

Active Proceedings

Expert conduct of the chosen process — arbitration, mediation, determination, or dispute board proceedings.

Step 04

Resolution

Delivery of final awards, determinations, or facilitated settlements, enabling closure and resumption of operations.

Principal

Neil Kirkpatrick

Chartered Quantity Surveyor, Chartered Construction Manager, Arbitrator, Adjudicator, Expert Determiner, Mediator, and Expert Witness with over twenty-five years of experience in construction disputes valued in excess of $150 million across Australia, Asia, and the United Kingdom.

Neil holds a Master of Laws (LL.M.) in International Commercial Law with Distinction and a Diploma in International Commercial Arbitration (DipICArb). He is accredited as an arbitrator, mediator, expert determiner, and adjudicator across multiple jurisdictions.

  • Fellow, Chartered Institute of Building (FCIOB) — Chartered Construction Manager
  • Fellow, Resolution Institute (FRI) — Chair, NSW Determinative Committee
  • Resolution Institute Accredited Arbitrator (Grade 2)
  • Advanced Dispute Board Member Certificate (DRBF)
  • Founding Member & Chairman, Construction Arbitration Centre Asia Pacific
