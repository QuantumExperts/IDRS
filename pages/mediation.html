Dispute Resolution Services

Commercial Mediation

Expert construction mediation facilitating confidential, voluntary resolution of complex disputes.

Construction Mediation with Technical Expertise

IDRS offers construction mediation services conducted by an experienced professional qualified as a Chartered Quantity Surveyor, Chartered Builder, and Chartered Construction Manager with over twenty years of experience in the construction industry. This deep technical background enables the mediator to understand the substance of disputes at a granular level, facilitating more informed and productive negotiations between the parties.

Confidentiality Protection

Mediation conducted by IDRS is completely confidential. Discussions during the mediation process cannot be disclosed to courts or used in any subsequent proceedings. This protection enables parties to negotiate freely and explore settlement options without concern that their positions may be used against them should the matter proceed to formal adjudication.

Cost and Time Efficiency

Construction disputes mediated through IDRS typically resolve faster and at lower cost than litigation or arbitration. The process often concludes within weeks rather than the extended timeframes associated with formal proceedings, enabling the parties to achieve resolution and resume normal business operations with minimal delay.

Voluntary Settlement

The parties maintain control over the outcome throughout the mediation process. The mediator facilitates negotiation rather than imposing a decision, resulting in higher compliance rates and outcomes that reflect the commercial interests and priorities of both parties.

Site Visits

The mediator may conduct on-site assessments prior to formal sessions, and in appropriate cases, the mediation itself may be held at the dispute location to facilitate understanding of the technical issues.

Joint and Private Sessions

The mediation process combines joint meetings with both parties and individual caucuses, allowing the mediator to explore each party's interests and identify potential areas of agreement.

Flexible Approach

Each mediation process is customised to accommodate the specific needs and dynamics of the dispute, ensuring the most productive environment for negotiation and resolution.

Expert Qualifications

The mediator holds a Master of Laws in International Commercial Law, a Diploma in International Commercial Arbitration, and accreditation across multiple dispute resolution panels.

Geographic Availability

IDRS mediation services operate internationally across Australia, Asia, and the United Kingdom. The mediator's familiarity with diverse legal frameworks and commercial practices across these jurisdictions enables effective facilitation of disputes arising under a wide range of contractual and regulatory regimes.
