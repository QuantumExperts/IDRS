Jurisdictional Considerations

Jurisdictions

Dispute resolution frameworks and institutional considerations across the Asia-Pacific region and beyond.

International Dispute Resolution Practice

IDRS provides dispute resolution services across sixteen jurisdictions spanning the Asia-Pacific, Europe, and North America. Services include commercial arbitration, expert determination, mediation, security of payment adjudication, dispute boards, expedited arbitration, and conciliation. Each jurisdiction is governed by distinct legislative frameworks, institutional rules, and procedural requirements, and an understanding of the applicable legal and institutional landscape is essential to the effective conduct of any dispute resolution process.

Select a jurisdiction below to view the detailed legislative frameworks, institutional considerations, and available dispute resolution mechanisms.

Asia-Pacific Jurisdictions

Australia
International Arbitration Act 1974 (Cth), ACICA Rules, Resolution Institute, state security of payment legislation
Hong Kong
Arbitration Ordinance Cap. 609, HKIAC Administered Arbitration Rules, Mediation Ordinance Cap. 620
Singapore
International Arbitration Act (Cap. 143A), SIAC Rules, SIMC, Singapore Convention on Mediation
Malaysia
Arbitration Act 2005, AIAC Arbitration Rules 2026, CIPAA 2012, Mediation Act 2012
China
Arbitration Law 1994, CIETAC Rules 2024, Belt and Road dispute resolution
India
Arbitration and Conciliation Act 1996, MCIA, DIAC, Commercial Courts Act 2015
Indonesia
Law No. 30 of 1999, BANI Rules 2025, Construction Services Law 2017
Japan
Arbitration Act 2003, JCAA Rules, JIDRC, Construction Dispute Review Committees
Philippines
ADR Act 2004 (RA 9285), PDRCI Rules, CIAC, Executive Order 1008
South Korea
Korean Arbitration Act, KCAB International Arbitration Rules, construction dispute mechanisms
Taiwan
Arbitration Act, CAA Arbitration Rules, Public Construction Commission
Thailand
Arbitration Act B.E. 2545, THAC Rules, Thai Arbitration Institute
Vietnam
Law on Commercial Arbitration 2010, VIAC Rules, Construction Law 2014

Europe & North America
