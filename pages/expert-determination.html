Expert Determination

Efficient, confidential, and cost-effective dispute resolution through independent technical expertise.

Efficient Resolution Through Independent Expertise

Expert determination uses an independent expert knowledgeable in the dispute's subject matter. The appointed expert provides a determination that may be legally binding based on agreed-upon rules. The process offers confidentiality and serves as an efficient, cost-effective means for resolving technical disputes without recourse to formal litigation or arbitration.

A Flexible Approach to Technical Disputes

This form of alternative dispute resolution is particularly well-suited to the construction industry and commonly appears in contract dispute resolution clauses. It is appropriate for variation disputes and technical issues where subject-matter expertise is more material than questions of law. Applications extend to rent reviews, insurance matters, and boundary disputes. The private, rules-based nature of the process provides informality and flexibility that parties increasingly value.

Binding and Non-Binding Options

The expert applies expertise to submitted issues and renders a determination. Whether the determination is binding depends on the contractual terms and the rules agreed between the parties. Some determinations are binding upon the parties; others are non-binding advisory opinions that the parties may accept or reject at their discretion. This flexibility accommodates parties seeking definitive resolution as well as those requiring independent expert guidance.

Technical Valuation

Variation valuations, contract interpretation, technical compliance matters, engineering standards, and contractual mechanism administration.

Project Management Issues

Delay, time extensions, programme sequencing, and methodology disputes; understanding scheduling and the implications of change on project delivery.

Defective Work Analysis

Construction defects, specification non-compliance, design failures, and remediation cost determination across all building types and infrastructure projects.

Efficiency and Speed

Faster, lower-cost resolution than litigation or formal arbitration; flexible, tailored processes designed to minimise disruption to ongoing operations.

Service Availability

IDRS expert determination services are available nationally throughout Australia and internationally across the Asia-Pacific region. The process is designed to provide expedient, cost-effective, and independent resolution, enabling businesses to resume normal operations with minimal delay.

Appointment and Administration

IDRS accepts appointments via direct party agreement or through embedded contractual clauses. Procedural fairness is maintained throughout the process whilst preserving the efficiency and confidentiality that characterise this form of resolution.

International Flexibility

The practice spans domestic and international disputes, drawing upon familiarity with various legal frameworks and contract types across multiple jurisdictions in Australia, Asia, and the United Kingdom.

Confidentiality and Privacy

Expert determination conducted by IDRS remains private throughout. Unlike litigation or arbitration, determinations are not made public, thereby protecting the commercial sensitivities and reputations of the parties involved.
